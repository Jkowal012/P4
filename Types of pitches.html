Types of Pitches

FastBall

Meant to disorient the batter with its pure speed but a bad release can cause more harm then good.

Curveball

Tricks the batter into thinking the batter is going one way and then shifts its tragectory in the last second

Sinker

Looks like a regular Fastball untill the ball crashes down into the ground in the last second

Slider

Makes the ball come close to the batter and difficult for them to get any power on their swing. As an added bonus, the ball looks like it might hit the batter making them more nervous about getting hit then hitting back
