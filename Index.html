Welcome!

Often reffered to as America's pasttime, baseball is an amazing sport with one very important position. The pitcher is the player responsible for putting the ball in play and making sure that the other team is unable to hit the ball. In the beggining, the pitches were simple but as time went on they have become physics defying feats of strength.

Did you Know?
  • The fastest pitch in MLB history was thrown by Aroldis Chapman of the New York Yankees. "The Cuban Missle" threw an unbelievable 105.1 MPH
  • The pitching mound was traditionally 60 feet and 6 inches but was moved to 61 feet and 6 inches in the 2021 season.
  • William Howard Thaft was the first president to throw the ceremonial first pitch.
  • The longest game of baseball lasted 8 hours and 6 minutes
  • The shortest? Only 31 minutes!
