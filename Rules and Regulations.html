Rules and Regulations

How to play

The Pitchers
    • Pitch must be thrown over home plate for it to be considered a strike
    • Three strikes and you are OUT!
    • Balls not thrown over home plate and that are not swung on are called balls
    • 2 different types of pitchers: starters and closers
    • Starters are commonly used for 100 pitches or when starting to slip up
    • Relievers may be called upon to finish the entire game or throw a single pitch
The Batters
    • Batters bat either from the left or right side
    • If the batter swings and does not make contact it is a strike irrelevant where the ball was thrown
    • Ball must land on floor to be considered a hit
